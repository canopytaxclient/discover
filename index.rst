Why Use Online Login?
--------------------

The official login page allows you to:

- View your current balance and available credit instantly.
- Make payments quickly without visiting a branch.
- Track rewards and cashback points in real-time.
- Review recent transactions and account activity.
- Update personal and security settings.
- Receive alerts and notifications directly.

---

Step-by-Step Guide to Logging In
--------------------------------

1. **Open your browser**  
   Go to `www.discovercard.com <https://www.discovercard.com>`_.

2. **Click "Sign In"**  
   The Sign In button is at the top-right corner.

3. **Enter your Username**  
   Type the username you created during registration.

4. **Enter your Password**  
   Remember: passwords are case-sensitive.

5. **Sign In**  
   Click the Sign In button to access your account dashboard.

6. **Optional: Remember Me**  
   Enable this if you are using a personal device for faster future logins.

---

Forgot Your Password?
--------------------

Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page, enter your email or username, follow the instructions to reset your password, then log in with your new password.

---

Mobile Login Tips
-----------------

- Use any smartphone or tablet browser.
- Download the **Discover Mobile App** for alerts and easy access.
- Log out after using public/shared devices.
- Use biometric login if supported for added convenience.
